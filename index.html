Frequently Asked Question

How do I schedule an appointment?

Visit our website or call our helpline to schedule appointments with our specialists. Walk-ins are also welcome for urgent care.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Check our website or contact our billing department for specific details on accepted insurance providers.

Are virtual consultations available?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for certain specialties. Contact our reception for more information and to schedule a virtual appointment.

What should I bring for my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, and any relevant medical records or test results. New patients may fill out forms available on our website.

How do I access my medical records?

Patients can access their medical records through our secure online portal. For assistance, contact our medical records department.

What emergency services do you provide?

Our hospital offers 24/7 emergency care, including trauma care, and our ambulance services ensure prompt response for critical situations.
